About us

Founded in 2016, Swadisht began as a humble kitchen inspired by the timeless recipes of Indian households. Today, we continue to serve the same soulful flavors with a touch of elegance. Our mission is to make every bite a journey — rich in culture, warmth, and unforgettable taste.
